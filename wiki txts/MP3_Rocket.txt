 is a YouTube downloader and MP3 converter that allows users to download videos and music from YouTube.


The MP3 Rocket software project was started in November 2005 by MP3 Rocket Inc. MP3 Rocket was first released in December 2005 as a  network application.
In January 2011 MP3 Rocket closed its P2P service and converted its software into a YouTube downloader.
MP3 Rocket comes in two versions, a freely downloadable basic version and the MP3 Rocket Pro
